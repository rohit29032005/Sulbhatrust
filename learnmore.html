About This Event

Join us for our most anticipated event of the year! Our Annual Day Celebration is a heartwarming showcase of the incredible talents, achievements, and progress of our special education students.

This year's theme focuses on "Celebrating Unique Abilities" and will feature performances, award ceremonies, and recognition of outstanding achievements in various categories.
